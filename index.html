Who are we?

There is no we in ozerogames (o0) - this is an imaginary entity for a one-man gamedev hobby.

So who am I?

My name is Matej, I like to think about making games and I'd love to actually make them. You can find me on twitter or github.

What kind of games do you make?

For now, you can find here simple casual games, primarily for mobile. See the Games section in the menu above. I'd like work on more complex games eventually, but getting things done is my priority right now.

I want to know more!

Follow "us" on twitter (@ozerogames) to get news and updates. For detailed progress (and some offtopic) you can follow me on my personal twitter (@rplnt). Feel free to ask any questions there, or if you prefer email, you can find it in the Contacts section.
